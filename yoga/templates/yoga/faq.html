Many people might ask, can you do yoga during your period? Well, the answer is yes, yoga during your period can be beneficial, especially if you’re experiencing uncomfortable symptoms. One can practise Cobbler's Pose (Baddha Konasana), Head to Knee Pose (Janu Sirsasana), Seated Straddle (Upavistha Konasana), Seated Forward Bend (Paschimottanasana), Supported Bridge Pose. Goddess Pose (Supta Baddha Konasana) so that you can get relief from cramps.
